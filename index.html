Meet Christian

Built on Craft,
Driven by Results

Christian is an Ohio native who brought his work ethic and passion for the trade to Eugene, Oregon in 2017. Since 2018, he's built a diverse background spanning new construction, residential service, and remodeling — from tight crawlspaces to complete bathroom overhauls.

What sets Christian apart isn't just skill — it's tenacity. He's the plumber who stays until the problem is solved, not until the job gets hard. When he's not on the job, you'll find him catching live music at a local venue or growing sunflowers in his garden.
